Prohibited Articles:
For safety reasons, some articles listed below, must not be carried in the bus or in the passenger's baggage. 
If any passenger is found to be in possession of such articles he / she will be asked to dispose of the same immediately or leave the bus:

Flammable fluids / liquids and solids such as lighter refills, lighter fuel, match, paint, thinner, fire-fighter fuel, matches
